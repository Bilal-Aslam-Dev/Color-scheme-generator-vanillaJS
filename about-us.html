About us

Intelligent Agents

We are proud to be forging a new path into the alternative financing technology boom with state of the art solutions and programs driven by artificial intelligence and advance data analytics. By relegating processes to computers and out of the hands of humans we create “intelligent agents” to analyze data, solve problems and predict outcomes more accurately.

Our Management

Our Management

Our management team possess decades of experience as successful hands-on business operators and corporate finance and technology executives. We always engage with our customers. Our experienced professionals who are subject matter experts in their specific fields provides customers with a valuable asset by assisting with business challenges. We are a business partner not just a capital provider.

01

Our Goal

Our goal is creating efficiencies for the way small businesses quickly secure funding for their needs through utilization of our adaptive modern technologies. The alternative financing market is all about speed, accuracy, predictability in every aspect of the process. Using our solutions, we shorten underwriting times and create unique hassle-free customer experiences while maximizing our profits and returns on investment.

02

Virtual Business

Virtual businesses are all about customer experience as the physical face-to-face aspects are often missing. While we embrace technology, we do recognize the requirement for the human factor lacking in much of today’s world. We maximize the use of technology for the back end of the business providing more time for our employees to engage with each and every customer. We are all about personal relationships with all customers both large and small.

03

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with exceptional guidance and service, which is timely and efficient. We empathize with the challenges financing small to midsize businesses. Banks are all about saying no when you need it most and creating a stressful situation on where to turn. The business operator is not often an expert on finance and simply filling out applications without having a plan can be problematic.
